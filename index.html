    Statistics

    • Odds to win Grand Prize are 1 in 292,201,338.00.
    • White balls are drawn from a pool of 69 balls and there can be no duplicates. The powerball is drawn from a pool of 26 balls. You must match all white balls in any order and the powerball to win the Grand Prize.
    • Math: Combination without Repetition. (69!)/(5!64!) * 26 = 292,201,338.00
